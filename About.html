About Me

I’m Henry Stephen Bdliya, a Computer Engineer and Systems Specialist who loves turning ideas into reality. With experience in web development, graphic design, and data-driven solutions, I focus on creating projects that are not just functional, but genuinely useful and meaningful for the people who use them.

Over the years, I’ve built experience in web development, graphic design, data management, and system optimization. I’ve trained students in Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript, helping them complete real portfolio projects, and conducted workshops for teachers to boost digital adoption in classrooms. I also embrace AI tools and data-driven solutions to improve efficiency and creativity, whether in project design, analytics, or educational initiatives.

Mentoring and teaching have always been central to my work. Guiding students and teams to develop their skills, confidence, and passion for technology is a key part of my mission.

Apart from technical projects and teaching, I enjoy writing, reading, and exploring creative ideas through sketching. I often take on personal projects to experiment with new technologies and find creative solutions to challenges. Collaborating with peers on community tech initiatives and workshops allows me to apply my skills in real-world settings while fostering teamwork and leadership. I’m constantly exploring new tools and methods to stay at the forefront of technology, and I enjoy sharing what I learn with others. These experiences reflect my commitment to continuous learning, creativity, and delivering meaningful impact through my work.
